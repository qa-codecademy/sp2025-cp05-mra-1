About Us

Since the beginning of the coronavirus pandemic, we have been your reliable partner for facility services (including all types of cleaning, property maintenance, moving, clearing and waste removal, painting, and repair work). Thanks to our experience and numerous satisfied clients, we enjoy excellent reviews and have established ourselves as a professional service provider on the market.

Our goal is to offer you a completely worry-free service – reliable, efficient, and of the highest quality. Whether it’s thorough cleaning with disinfection, professional property maintenance, or a stress-free move – we take care of everything with attention and expertise.

Let us convince you with our service! We’ll be happy to support you.
